About This Project

Actionist AI Transcript Analyzer
Developed as part of the Actionist's AI Operating Priority

Developed to enhance one of Actionist’s core capabilities - leveraging expert interviews to guide client strategy and execution. This tool represents a new class of proprietary, AI-enabled systems built to improve how consultants capture and use first-hand intelligence.

The assistant uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to extract, attribute, and organize verbatim quotations from interview transcripts. It connects OpenAI’s advanced models with Supabase vector storage to enable fast, transparent sourcing of qualitative evidence for use in analyses, reports, and client presentations.

Principles
• Operates within Actionist’s data confidentiality and ethical-use framework.
• Prioritizes verbatim accuracy and traceable source citation.
• Enhances consultant efficiency and analytical rigor.
• Avoids paraphrasing, interpretation, or fabrication.

This project demonstrates Actionist Consulting’s commitment to responsible, high-impact AI adoption to enhance consulting workflows.
